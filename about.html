The Journey & Educational Philosophy

Godwin Public School is a co-educational institution from Kindergarten to Matriculation, originated in 1997 and maintained by Shri Kalki Educational Trust for over 29 years. In academics, the school has been consistently producing cent percent (100%) results in SSLC exams and receiving awards every year from the Government of Karnataka.

Each class is filled with limited seats maintaining a student-teacher ratio of 20 : 1, ensuring individual attention to each student. Children with severe learning needs are given special attention, gradually integrating them into the mainstream curriculum.

  • Consistently producing 100% SSLC pass results with Government of Karnataka awards.
  • Strict 20 : 1 student-teacher ratio with individual care & inclusive learning support.
  • Concept-based teaching focused on developing social consciousness & moral integrity.
  • Annual Day performing arts celebrations at Bangalore's iconic Chowdaiah Memorial Hall.
