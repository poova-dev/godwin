Admissions Process

A seamless 5-step journey to becoming part of the Godwin family.

1

Initial Enquiry

Fill out our online form or visit the campus to collect the prospectus and admission kit.

2

Campus Interaction

Schedule a personalized tour of our facilities and meet with the academic leadership team.

3

Assessment & Interview

Students undergo a developmental assessment followed by a friendly interaction with the Principal.

4

Documentation

Submit the required academic records, birth certificate, and residency proof for verification.

5

Confirmation

On approval, pay the admission fee to secure your child's seat for the upcoming session.

Frequently Asked Questions

What transport facilities are available? expand_more

We provide a GPS-enabled fleet of buses covering major routes in Bengaluru. Each bus is accompanied by a trained female attendant and follows strict safety protocols. Parents can track the bus location in real-time through our mobile app.

Are school uniforms provided on campus? expand_more

Yes, uniforms can be measured and ordered directly from the school's on-campus store during the admission window. We provide regular daily uniforms, house-colored sports kits, and winter sweaters.

What meal options are available for students? expand_more

We offer an optional nutritious vegetarian meal plan prepared in our hygienic central kitchen. The menu is designed by a nutritionist to provide a balanced diet, including breakfast and a full lunch. Special dietary needs can be accommodated upon request.

What is the student-teacher ratio? expand_more

We maintain a strict student-teacher ratio of 20:1 — limited seats per class — ensuring every child receives special personal attention. Children with severe learning needs are given additional focused support to gradually integrate into mainstream learning.
